How to save your data

Your game data is saved in this browser only. To keep it safe, back it up regularly using one of the options below.

Think of it like saving a document — your data only exists here until you back it up.

Email backup — easiest on mobile

1Tap Email backup at the top of the app
2Your Mail app opens with the backup attached and ready to send
3Send it to yourself — it lands in your inbox as a safe copy
4To restore, open that email on any device, tap the attachment, then tap Import in the app

Export — save to your device

1Tap Export — a file downloads called soccer_backup_YYYYMMDD.json
2On iPhone, it saves to the Files app → Downloads. Move it to iCloud or share it to be safe

Add to home screen — use it like an app

1In Safari, tap the Share button (the box with the arrow)
2Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
3Tap Add — the app icon appears on your home screen and opens fullscreen
