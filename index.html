How to save your data

Your data is saved in this browser only. Use Export regularly to keep a backup — it saves all players and all their data in one file.

Think of it like saving a document — your data only exists here until you export it.

Export — saving your data

1Click Export at the top — a JSON file downloads
2Move it somewhere safe — iCloud, Google Drive, or Dropbox
3Do this regularly — after every few games

Import — restoring your data

1Click Import and select your .json backup file
2All players and data are restored — duplicates skipped automatically
